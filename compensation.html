How you're paid

Three income streams,
paid out monthly.

1

Retail margin

Keep 25% of every sale you make directly through your personal storefront, paid the moment the order ships.

2

Team bonus

From Senior Partner up, earn a percentage of your team's combined monthly volume — the bigger and healthier your team, the higher the bonus.

3

Profit-share pool

Ambassadors share in a company-wide profit pool distributed quarterly, on top of every other stream below it.
